=== DARK MODE UI PALETTE (GREY & BLUE) ===

-- BACKGROUNDS --
• Main Canvas: #121212 (Deep neutral grey; reduces eye strain)
• Surface/Cards: #1E1E26 (Blue-tinted grey; creates elevation/depth)
• Sidebar/Header: #18181F (Mid-tone for navigation structural contrast)

-- INTERACTIVE & ACCENTS --
• Primary Accent: #0078FF (Electric blue for buttons & active states)
• Hover State: #3391FF (Lighter blue for interactive feedback)
• Border/Divider: #33333D (Subtle grey for sectioning and card outlines)

-- TYPOGRAPHY --
• Heading/Primary: #E0E0E0 (Cool off-white; high legibility)
• Body/Secondary: #A0A0AB (Muted grey-blue for less critical info)
• Disabled Text: #62626C (Low-contrast grey)

-- STATUS INDICATORS --
• Success: #00C853 (Vibrant green for "online" or "complete")
• Warning: #FFAB00 (Amber for alerts or pending states)
• Error: #FF5252 (Muted red for failures or critical stops)

-- SYSTEM SHADOWS --
• Soft Glow: rgba(0, 120, 255, 0.15) (Subtle blue glow for active cards)
